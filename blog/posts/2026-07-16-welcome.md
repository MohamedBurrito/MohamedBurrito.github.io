This is the first post on the blog. Longer-form notes, write-ups, and
thoughts on offensive security will land here — separate from the
structured Case Files section.

## How this works

Posts are plain Markdown files with a front-matter block at the top
(title, date, tags, cover image, published flag). Drop images into
`/assets/blog/` and reference them like any other image:

```markdown
![Burp Suite repeater tab](../assets/blog/example.png)
```

Set `published: false` in the front-matter to keep a post as a draft —
it won't show up on the blog index until flipped to `true`.
